I am Nayanmoni, I have a deep passion for abstract structures and the underlying patterns that drive dynamic behavior in nature and computation. I am primarily interested in computational topology, manifold theory, dynamical systems, and chaos theory.

My current focus is on applying topological and geometric methods to study complex systems and nonlinear dynamics. I enjoy exploring how these abstract tools reveal deep insights in systems ranging from neural networks to fluid flows.
